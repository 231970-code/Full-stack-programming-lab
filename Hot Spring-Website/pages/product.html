Product Description

The Luxury Spa 4000 is designed to provide the ultimate relaxation experience. With its spacious seating for 4-6 people, advanced jet system, and modern features, this hot tub is perfect for both family enjoyment and entertaining guests.

Featuring 30 strategically placed hydrotherapy jets, our patented massage system targets key muscle groups to provide therapeutic relief and relaxation. The energy-efficient heating system ensures your spa is always ready when you are, while keeping operating costs low.
